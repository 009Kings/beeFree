Pollination Station

How to Play

Help Mr. Bee in his mission to pollinate as many flowers as he can! Use "W" to move up, "A" to move left, "S" to move down, and "D" to move right. Fly though the world and land on the flowers you pass.

Watch out for dangers, as the life of a bee can be short with only one stinger.
